How to download the android NullWeb app

Open your browser and go to https://www.nullmedia.infinityfreeapp.com/. Tap the three dots in the top-right corner of your browser (this is the "menu" button). In the dropdown menu, tap on "Add to Home Screen". You can enter a name for the shortcut, or you can leave it as the default name. Once you're ready, tap "Add". The web app icon will now appear on your home screen!
