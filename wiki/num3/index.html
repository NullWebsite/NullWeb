How to post images in NullMedia

Option one: find an image online. Step 1. Copy the following: <img src="your-image-source.jpg"><br> Paste that into the body text field. Step 2. Find an image, right click it, and click copy image address. Step 3. Go back to the body text field and double click the part that says linkhere and paste the image address. Post it, and you're done!
