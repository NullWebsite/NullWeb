NullMedia









Display Name: what you want it to say on the home screen


Payload Identifier: unique ID for your app (template: com.yourapp.webclip (probably needs to have 2 periods and com at the beginning and webclip at the end))


Base64 Icon: what your app will look like of the home screen. (find a image to Base64 converter online, upload a picture, copy the output (NOT INCLUDING THE data:image/png;base64, PART), and paste it in the text box)


Display Name: what website you want to appify (template: google.com (don't use google for this just get the actual app))


Organization: who is this by (template: [your name], appified by KingNullboy)


Profile Identifier: another unique ID for your app (template: com.yourapp.config (probably needs to have 2 periods and com at the beginning and config at the end))


Note that this only works on Safari, unfortunately, and that it won't always work as well as my website's app does.
